Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our academy

Our admission process begins with an online application form, followed by an entrance assessment and parent-student interview. We evaluate academic records, extracurricular activities, and overall fit with our school community. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis, and we encourage early submission.

We maintain a low student-teacher ratio of 15:1 to ensure personalized attention for each student. This allows our teachers to understand individual learning styles, provide targeted support, and foster meaningful relationships with students and families.

Yes! We offer over 50 extracurricular activities including sports teams, music ensembles, debate club, robotics, drama, art classes, and community service programs. Students are encouraged to explore their interests and develop well-rounded skills beyond academics.

School hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. We also offer extended care programs from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM for working parents. After-school enrichment programs and sports activities are available until 5:30 PM.

Yes, we offer need-based financial aid and merit scholarships to qualifying families. Approximately 30% of our students receive some form of financial assistance. We believe that financial constraints should not prevent deserving students from accessing quality education.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our academy

Our admission process includes an online application, entrance assessment, parent interview, and document submission. We accept applications year-round and conduct rolling admissions. Priority is given to early applicants.
We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention. Elementary classes have 20 students, middle school 15 students, and high school 12 students per class, all with dedicated teachers and teaching assistants.
Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid. Up to 30% of our students receive some form of financial assistance. Applications are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
We offer over 50 clubs and activities including sports teams, music ensembles, debate club, robotics, art classes, drama productions, community service programs, and more. All students are encouraged to participate.
Our graduates have a 98% college acceptance rate, with many attending top-tier universities. We provide comprehensive college counseling starting in 9th grade, including test preparation, application assistance, and scholarship guidance.
